About Us

THE FUTURE OF AI TECHNOLOGY IN MENTAL HEALTH IS NOT ABOUT REPLACING HUMANS BUT IT'S ABOUT SUPPORTING THEM.

As most of the teens and adults of this generation are facing mental health issues and daily go through suicidal thoughts, anxiety, self-doubt and they do not even open up to their friends and parents nowadays about it! Our idea is to build a technology where these people will live a stress/anxiety-free life! We will connect all the renowned Psychiatrist and Psychologist around the globe to these people who are going through such dilemmas and create an AI-based system to track their mental progress, design some routine and activities, schedule their sessions which will help them to cope up, AI that will talk to them, understand their feelings/emotions, give a solution and help them to get back to a new problem-free life!
